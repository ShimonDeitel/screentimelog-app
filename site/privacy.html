Privacy Policy

Screentime Log is an offline-first app. All data you enter is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party. We do not collect, sell, or share any personal information. There are no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no network requests made by this app.

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple's App Store / StoreKit. We never see or store your payment information.

If you delete the app, your locally stored data is removed along with it.
